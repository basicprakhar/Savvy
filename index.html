How does it work?

  • Topic Selection and Roadmap Access:

    Users begin by selecting a desired topic from a range of available learning paths on Savvy's platform. These topics cover various subjects and skills. Once a topic is chosen, users gain access to a structured roadmap specifically tailored to that area of study.

  • Structured Learning Paths:

    Within the selected roadmap, users embark on a structured learning journey. Each pathway consists of step-by-step modules or lessons. These modules are thoughtfully organized to guide users through the learning process, gradually building upon previous knowledge and skills.

  • Progress Tracking and Achievement Recognition:

    Savvy enables users to track their progress through the modules. Achievements are acknowledged with badges or certifications, motivating continuous learning and growth.
